About Us

SafeLine! provides top-quality fire extinguishers and safety equipment to protect homes and businesses. Our range includes standard and throwable extinguishers for all fire types.

Types of Fire Extinguishers

Water Extinguishers

Ideal for Class A fires (wood, paper). Cools burning materials.

Foam Extinguishers

For Class A and B (flammable liquids). Smothers flames.

CO2 Extinguishers

For electrical (Class E) and Class B. No residue.

Dry Powder

Multi-purpose for A, B, C, electrical. Stops fire spread.

Wet Chemical

For kitchen fires (Class F). Prevents re-ignition.

Throwables

Quick-deploy grenades for instant fire suppression.
